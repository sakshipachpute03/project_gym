About-us



At Khamkar's Fitness club we are committed to transforming your fitness journey into an inspiring and empowering experience. At the heart of fitness innovation, Khamkar Fitness club provides a holistic approach to health and wellness. Our dedicated team of certified personal trainers works closely with each client to design personalized workout plans tailored to your unique fitness goals. We've got you covered whether you're aiming for fat loss, muscle gain, or overall body conditioning. Nutrition plays a vital role in achieving the best results, and our experienced nutritionists are here to guide you every step of the way. From creating balanced meal plans to providing professional advice, we ensure you fuel your body the right way.


The studio opened on 27th January, 2025. Owner - Gaurav Londe. Currently working with a team consisting of 3 people including himself.

Gaurav Londe

Why Choose Us ?

1. Comprehensive Fitness Facilities

From cardio and cycling to weight lifting, strength training, and kickboxing, we provide a wide range of dedicated workout zones to cater to every fitness goal. Our state-of-the-art equipment ensures an effective and enjoyable workout experience.


2. Certified Male & Female Trainers

Our team of expert male and female trainers offer personalized guidance and create custom workout plans to help you achieve your fitness goals. Their expertise ensures safe and efficient training for individuals of all levels.


3. Modern Amenities for Comfort

We prioritize your convenience with fully-equipped changing rooms, secure lockers, Wi-Fi, and water dispensers. These amenities ensure a smooth, hassle-free workout experience every time you visit us.


4. Inclusive Environment for Everyone

At Khamkar's Fitness Club, we foster a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere for individuals of all fitness levels. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned athlete, you’ll find the support and motivation you need to succeed.
